The Stars of Switched At Birth

  • Vanessa Marano

    She plays Bay Kennish. She was switched at birth with Daphne Vasquez causing her to raised in Mission Hills with Katheryn, Jon, and Toby. She lived in wealthy circumstances unlike Daphne. Bay is the one that finds out that she isn't the biological daughter of the Kennishes through a school experiment. She noticed her blood type was not the same as her families, so she decided to take a DNA test. Later she meets Daphne and Emmett. She decides to learn ASL in order to be able to communicate with them and others that she meets alone the way. She also gets to meet Regina, her biological mother. Bay Kennish is an artist and she also does grafftii by tagging walls with her art work called Hammer Girl.

    Katie Leclerc

    She plays Daphne Paloma Vasquez who is part of the deaf community. She became part of the deaf community when she was 3 years old and lost her hearing to meningitis. When she was born, she was switched with Bay Kennish which led to her being raised by Regina Vasquez in East Riverside. She was raised with Latin Culture. When she was 8, she met Emmett Bledsoe, who defended Dahpne when she was being teased. Emmett introduced his mother, Melody Bledsoe to Regina and Daphne. Melody helped Regina learn ASL in order to be able to communicate with Daphne much easier.

    Sean Berdy

    He plays Emmett who is part of the deaf community. Emmett met Daphne when she was 8. He developed a crush on her at an early age. However, when he met bae, he began liking her too. His parents are Meoldy and Cameron Bledsoe, who are both deaf. Emmett inherited his deafness. He has an interest in photogrpahy. He is a talented drummer. He rides a motorcycle. Bay is the only hearing girl he has ever dated.

    Lea Thompson and D.W Moffett

    This is the Kennish Couple. They are married.They are the parents of Bay and the biological parents on Daphne. Lea Thompson plays Kathryn Kennish, an author and stay-at-home mother. D.W Moffett plays John Kennish, former third-baseman for the Kansas City Royals. After he retired from baseball, he runs a chain of Kennish Carwashes.

    Lucas Grabeel

    He plays Toby Kennish. He is the son of Kathyrn and John. He is older than Dahpne and Bay. At the beginning, Toby was an avid poker player, which got him into financial issues.He has a band called Guitar Face which Emmett joins at some point as their drummer.

    Constance Marie

    She plays Regina Vasquez. She is the legal mother of Daphne and the biological mother of Bay. Regina actually knew about the switch early in Daphne's childhood, but she didn't tell anyone. When Daphne became deaf, Melody was the one that taught her ASL and introduced her to the deaf community. She hired a private investigator to find Bay. Later in the series, Bay finds out that Regina knew all this time and hid it from them for 13 years. The Kennishes plan on taking Daphne and keeping Bay because they believe Regina isn't stable and that she is a bad parent. Persuaded by Bay, The Kennishes allow Regina and Daphne to move into the guest house in Mission Hills.
